What's the difference between Modules and Articles?

Modules :

Modules are a way to dive deep into a particular topic in astrophysics. Usually, they contain a lot of resources and links that guide the reader rather than the module itself serving as a source of knowledge. Moreover, mathematical analysis, problem-solving, etc. are highly appreciated in this section. For examples, do check out Astrovenutre’s module section!

Articles :

Articles are a great way to share knowledge and updated information on astrophysics. The main aim isn’t to dive really but to give readers something to chill with.
